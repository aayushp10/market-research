# Private Credit Defaults and Withdrawals (March 2026)

## Default Rate Data

- **Morgan Stanley forecast:** Private credit direct lending defaults could reach **8%** (vs historical average 2-2.5%)
- **Smaller issuers:** Currently experiencing **10.9%** default rate
- Characterization: "significant but not systemic" — transitions from "zero loss illusion" to conventional credit asset class

## Fund Withdrawal Data

- **Ares Strategic Income Fund** ($10.7B): 11.6% withdrawal requests, limited to 5%
- **Apollo Global Management:** Similar restrictions; 45% of requested withdrawals approved
- **Blue Owl Capital and Cliffwater:** Halted or limited withdrawals

## Mark-to-Market Pressure

- **Blackstone BCRED:** Down 0.4% in February (first monthly decline in three years)
- BCRED revised down loans including SaaS company Medallia
- **Apollo Debt Solutions BDC:** Software exposure exceeds 12%

## Sector Exposure

- Software sector exposure: approximately **26%** of direct lending portfolios (Morgan Stanley estimate)
- Total private credit sector size: **$3 trillion**
