# Distressed Software Leveraged Loans — January 2026

## Scale

- Record **$25 billion** in distressed software sector loans (below 80¢ threshold) as of end January 2026
- Volume more than doubled in January alone
- Software = **13%** market share in Morningstar LSTA US Leveraged Loan Index
- Software = **31%** of ALL distressed loans in the index (massively over-represented)

## Broader Distress Context

- Share of all outstanding loans priced below 80¢ rose to **6.4%** (highest since May 2023)
- Software loans fell **392 bps** in February 2026 to **87.64**
- **21%** of all out-of-court restructurings (LMEs) in 2025 involved software companies

## Private Credit Exposure

- Private credit software exposure estimated at **$600-750 billion** in loans
- Software exposure in direct lending estimated at roughly **26%** (Morgan Stanley)
- **$46.9 billion** already trading at distressed levels

## European Comparison

- European leveraged loan market fared better: software = 7% of index, only 2% of distressed
