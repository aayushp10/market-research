# The SaaSpocalypse: Private Credit Growth Plateau

## AI Disruption Thesis

- AI agents replacing human workers erodes the "sticky" recurring revenue model that underpinned SaaS valuations in private credit
- Per-seat licensing model fundamentally disrupted

## Valuation Impact

- Tech-heavy portfolios experienced valuation reductions of **15-20%**
- Blue Owl's OTIC faced redemption requests of nearly 41%

## Industry-Wide Distress

- Private credit AUM: **$3.5 trillion** as of early 2026
- Default rates: climbed to **5.8%**, highest level on record (April 2026)
- Maturity wall: over **$620 billion** in debt looming
- BDC fundraising: declined **40% year-over-year**

## Peer Redemption Data

- **Blackstone BCRED:** $3.8 billion redemption requests (8% of NAV)
- **Apollo Global Management:** 11.2% redemption requests, exceeding 5% quarterly cap

## Forward Risk

- Default rates could "peak as high as 13-15% by late 2026" if AI disruption continues at current pace
