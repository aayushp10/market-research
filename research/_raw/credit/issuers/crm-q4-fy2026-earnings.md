# Salesforce Q4 FY2026 Earnings

**Reported:** February 25, 2026 (fiscal year ended January 31, 2026)

## Full Year FY2026

- **Revenue:** $41.5B (+10% YoY, +9% constant currency), includes $399M Informatica contribution
- **GAAP operating margin:** 20.1%
- **Non-GAAP operating margin:** 34.1%
- **Operating cash flow:** $15.0B (+15% YoY)
- **Free cash flow:** $14.4B (+16% YoY)
- **RPO:** $72.4B (+14% YoY)
- **cRPO:** $35.1B (+16% YoY)
- **Shareholder returns:** >$14B via buybacks and dividends

## Q4 FY2026

- **Revenue:** $11.2B (+12% YoY, +10% constant currency)
- **Operating cash flow:** $5.46B
- **Free cash flow:** $5.32B
- **Adjusted EPS:** $3.81 (vs $3.05 estimate)
- **Net income:** $1.94B (+14% YoY)

## AI & Data

- **AI & Data revenue:** >$2.9B (+200% YoY)
- **Agentforce ARR:** $800M (+169% YoY)
- **Agentforce deals closed:** 29,000+ (+50% QoQ)
- **Agentic Work Units delivered:** 2.4B (+57% QoQ)

## FY2027 Guidance

- **Revenue:** $45.8–$46.2B (10–11% growth)
- **FY2030 target:** $63B long-term revenue goal

## Capital Allocation Announcement

Alongside earnings, CRM announced a $50B share repurchase program and ~6% dividend increase.
