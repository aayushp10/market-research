# Salesforce Bond Sale Demand Dynamics

## Order Book

- **Final orders:** $36B for $25B offering → **1.4x oversubscription**
- **Comparison:** Amazon's concurrent $37B deal drew $126B in orders (3.4x coverage)
- Weakest reception for a major IG tech bond deal in recent memory

## Spread Performance

- 40-year tranche: priced at 185bps over Treasuries, tightened only 10bps from initial talk
- Typical IG new issue tightening in 2026: ~30bps
- Significantly less tightening than market standard → weak demand signal
- Front-end tranches (2028–2031) attracted more interest; PMs found "the front end as reasonably attractive"

## Investor Concerns Cited

1. **AI disruption risk:** Christian Hoffmann (Thornburg Investments): "The hyperscalers are building the infrastructure that is creating the existential threat to companies like Salesforce."
2. **Debt-funded buyback:** Using leverage for shareholder returns, not investment
3. **Stock performance:** CRM shares down >25% in 2026 YTD as of pricing date
4. **Duration risk:** 40-year bonds to a company facing secular AI disruption questions
