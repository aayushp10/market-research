# OTIC Q1 2026 Redemption Event

## Redemption Data

- OTIC received tender requests totaling **40.7% of outstanding shares** in Q1 2026 (vs 15.4% in Q4 2025)
- Blue Owl capped repurchases at **5% on a pro rata basis**
- Tender offer honored **$179 million** in redemptions
- Gross capital inflows: **$127 million**
- Net outflows: **$52 million** (less than 2% of NAV)
- Approximately **$1 billion** in unfulfilled redemption requests remain

## Fund Metrics

- NAV: approximately **$3 billion**
- Portfolio fair value: **$6.2 billion** across **190 companies** and **41 end markets**
- Liquidity: over **$1.3 billion** (cash + borrowing capacity) as of February 28, 2026
- Net leverage: **0.82x** debt-to-equity

## Management Commentary

- Attributed elevated redemptions to "negative sentiment toward the non-traded business development company sector and concerns about AI-related disruption to software companies"
- Also cited "concentrated shareholder base in certain wealth channels and regions"
- Blue Owl said there was a "meaningful disconnect" between public sentiment on private credit funds and the underlying assets

## OWL Equity Impact

- Blue Owl Capital (OWL) shares fell **9%** on the announcement
- Stock at approximately **$8.54**, down **56%** over 52 weeks
