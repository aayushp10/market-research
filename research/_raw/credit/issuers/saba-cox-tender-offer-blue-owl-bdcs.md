# Saba Capital / Cox Capital Tender Offer for Blue Owl BDCs

## Targets

- Blue Owl Capital Corporation II (OBDC II)
- Blue Owl Technology Income Corp. (OTIC)
- Blue Owl Credit Income Corp. (OCIC)

## Offer Terms

- **Price:** 20-35% discount to most recent estimated NAV and dividend reinvestment price
- **Notice:** Submitted to OBDC II on February 17, 2026; subsequent notices to OTIC and OCIC
- **Timeline:** Formal commencement after 10-business-day notice period for each BDC

## Rationale

- Saba/Cox aim to "provide a liquidity solution to retail investors in the wake of a significant industry-wide increase in BDC redemption requests, multiple quarters of net outflows and a rise in redemption gate provisions"

## Scale

- Offered amounts characterized as "relatively small amount of the total outstanding shares"
- "May not provide a meaningful liquidity solution for all investors"

## Significance

Third-party activist intervention signals deep discount to NAV is perceived as durable. Willingness to buy at 65-80¢ on the dollar implies Saba sees trapped value behind the gate, or sees the gate itself as the trade.
