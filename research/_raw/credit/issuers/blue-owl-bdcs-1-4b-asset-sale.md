# Blue Owl BDCs $1.4 Billion Asset Sale

## Transaction Structure

- **Total:** $1.4 billion in assets sold to four leading North American public pension and insurance investors
- **OBDC II:** $600 million (34% of its total investment commitments)
- **OTIC:** $400 million (6% of its total investment commitments)
- **OBDC:** $400 million (2% of its total investment commitments)

## Pricing

- Sold at fair value: **99.7% of par value** as of February 12, 2026
- Kroll, LLC provided fairness opinions for each BDC

## Portfolio Characteristics

- **97% senior secured** debt investments
- **128 distinct portfolio companies** across **27 industries**
- Average investment size: **$5 million**
- Largest industry: internet software and services (13%)

## Use of Proceeds

- OBDC II: return of capital distribution (~$2.35/share, ~30% of NAV) plus debt paydown
- OTIC and OBDC: debt paydown and capital deployment
- Expected completion: Q1 2026

## Key Quote

Craig Packer, CEO: "This transaction underscores the confidence that large, experienced buyers have in our direct lending platform."
