**Total:** $25B face value. **Net proceeds:** ~$24.885B.

All notes are fixed rate, senior, unsecured, unsubordinated.

## Use of Proceeds

Entire net proceeds fund a $25B accelerated share repurchase (ASR).

### ASR Details

- Initial delivery: ~80% of shares on March 16, 2026
- Final settlement: Q4 2026
- Pricing: average daily VWAP during transaction term
- Counterparties: Banco Santander, Bank of America, Citibank, JPMorgan Chase, Morgan Stanley

## Term Loan Refinancing

- New: $6B five-year senior unsecured term loan
- Replaces: existing $4B 364-day facility + $2B three-year facility
- Rate: alternate base rate or term SOFR + applicable margin
- Prepayable without penalty

## Underwriters

J.P. Morgan Securities, BofA Securities, Barclays Capital, Citigroup Global Markets, Wells Fargo Securities.

## Pre-Existing Bond Debt

~$8.5B outstanding from 2021 Slack acquisition financing (originally $8B across maturities from 2024 to 2061; the $1B 2024 notes have matured).
