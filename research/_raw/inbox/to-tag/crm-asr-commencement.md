# Salesforce ASR Commencement — March 16, 2026

## Key Details

- **ASR size:** $25B — largest single accelerated share repurchase in history
- **Initial delivery:** ~103 million shares (~80% of total anticipated repurchase based on March 11, 2026 closing price)
- **Final settlement:** Expected Q3 or Q4 FY2027 (calendar Q4 2026 or Q1 2027)
- **Pricing mechanism:** Average daily VWAP during transaction term
- **Counterparties:** Banco Santander, Bank of America, Citibank, JPMorgan Chase, Morgan Stanley
- **Funding:** Entire net proceeds of $25B senior notes offering (priced March 11, settled March 13)

## Total Buyback Program

- **Total authorization:** $50B aggregate Share Repurchase Program (Board-authorized February 2026)
- **Executed portion:** $25B via ASR (first half)
- **Remaining:** $25B — may proceed through additional ASR or traditional open market purchases; no disclosed timeline

## Management Commentary

Marc Benioff, Chair and CEO: "We are aggressively repurchasing shares because we are so confident in the future of Salesforce."

Robin Washington, President and COO/CFO: "This $25 billion ASR reflects our increased conviction in the durability of our growth and cash flow trajectory."

## Capital Returns Context

- FY2026 total shareholder returns: >$14B (buybacks + dividends)
- Quarterly dividend increased ~6% to $0.44/share alongside the buyback announcement
