# OTIC — Blue Owl Technology Income Corp

> Non-traded BDC sponsored by Blue Owl Capital, focused on senior secured lending to technology and software companies. In monitoring coverage due to read-across to core names OBDC and OTF (same sponsor) and as the primary data point for the non-traded BDC redemption stress theme.

- **Non-accrual rate:** 0.0% (as of Q4 2024 per KBRA). [[research/_raw/credit/issuers/kbra-otic-bbb-stable-apr-2025|KBRA rating]].

### Redemption history

- **Q4 2025 (tender ending January 8, 2026):** 15.4% of shares requested. Blue Owl increased offer from 5% to ~19%, satisfying 100% of requests. Total: $527.2 million redeemed. [[research/_raw/credit/issuers/otic-q1-2026-redemption-caps|Q1 2026 redemption data]].
- **Q1 2026 (tender March 3–31, 2026):** 40.7% of shares requested — historic industry high. Capped at 5%, honoring $179 million. Net outflows: $52 million (<2% of NAV). ~$1 billion in unfulfilled requests. [[research/_raw/credit/issuers/otic-q1-2026-redemption-caps|Q1 2026 redemption data]].

## Mark validation notes

- KBRA last affirmed April 2025 on Q4 2024 data — marks pre-date the February 2026 software loan sell-off (LSTA Software sub-index declined ~392bp in February alone). [[research/_raw/credit/themes/distressed-software-loans-record-25b|Software distress data]].
- The $1.4B asset sale at 99.7% of par (February 2026) provides a partial external mark validation for the subset of assets sold. However, this was 6% of OTIC's commitments — not representative of the full portfolio. [[research/_raw/credit/issuers/blue-owl-bdcs-1-4b-asset-sale|$1.4B asset sale]].
- Zero non-accruals as of Q4 2024 (KBRA), but this predates the February–March 2026 software distress wave. Non-accrual status as of Q1 2026 is not yet disclosed.
- Software sector represents ~41.5% of portfolio by end-market classification. With $25B of software loans industry-wide now below 80¢, mark staleness risk is elevated. [[research/_raw/credit/themes/distressed-software-loans-record-25b|Software distress data]].
