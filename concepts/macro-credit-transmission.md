# Macro-Credit Transmission

The channels through which macro shocks (oil price spikes, rate moves, geopolitical events) transmit into credit spread movements. The key analytical discipline is identifying which transmission channel dominates and at what threshold: an oil move below $100 is a sector-dispersion story (energy winners vs. losers); above $110 it becomes a stagflationary demand shock affecting the entire index.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Correctly identifying the active transmission channel determines whether you trade sector dispersion or index direction.
