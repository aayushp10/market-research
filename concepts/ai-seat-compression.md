# AI Seat Compression

The dynamic where AI agents and automation reduce the number of human workers performing a function, directly compressing the per-seat software licenses required. If AI enables five workers' output from one, the software vendor loses four seats of recurring revenue. This threatens the unit economics of traditional SaaS per-seat pricing models and is the core mechanism behind the 2026 software sector repricing.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Seat compression is the specific transmission mechanism from AI disruption to enterprise software revenue — it converts abstract disruption risk into quantifiable revenue headwinds for credit analysis.
