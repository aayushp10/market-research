# Fallen Angel Framework

The analytical framework for identifying and timing IG credits at risk of downgrade to HY (fallen angel events). Key inputs: cross-agency rating trajectory, deleveraging timeline credibility, fundamental earnings trajectory, and the pre-event spread dynamics where IG holders trim ahead of forced selling at crossover.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Fallen angel timing drives some of the largest dislocations in credit markets; the spread move happens before the downgrade, not after.
