# Energy Sub-Industry Decomposition

The analytical requirement to decompose "Energy IG" into its constituent sub-industries (Integrated, E&P, Refiners, Gas Pipes, Chems, Airlines) whenever oil moves materially. Each sub-industry has a different relationship to crude price: E&P benefits directly, refiners depend on crack spreads, chems face feedstock pressure, airlines face jet fuel costs.

**Why it matters in this framework:** The relative value trade in energy credit almost always sits inside the complex, not between energy and rest-of-IG; reporting energy as a single sector masks the actionable signal.
