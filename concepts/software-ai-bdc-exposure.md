# Software/AI BDC Exposure

The concentration risk from BDC portfolios heavily weighted toward software and SaaS companies. Filing-level industry breakdowns can mask true software exposure by splitting it across 30+ sub-industries. When the LSTA Software sub-index moves, the entire book reprices.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Software concentration is the single largest sector risk factor in tech-focused BDCs; a sector credit event becomes a whole-book event.
