# Rating Migration

The process by which a credit issuer's rating changes over time, tracked through rating actions (upgrades, downgrades, outlook changes, watch placements) across the three major agencies. Migration risk is highest for names at rating boundaries (BBB-/BB+ crossover) where a one-notch move triggers index reconstitution and forced selling.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Migration risk at the IG/HY boundary is the most consequential credit event for portfolio construction; index inclusion/exclusion drives flows that overwhelm fundamentals.
