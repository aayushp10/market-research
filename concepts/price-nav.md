# Price/NAV

The ratio of a BDC's market price to its reported net asset value per share. For public BDCs, Price/NAV reflects the market's assessment of mark quality, NII sustainability, and management credibility. Discounts widen when the market believes NAV is overstated.

**Why it matters in this framework:** P/NAV is the primary valuation metric for BDCs; headline discounts can be misleading when adjusted for mark staleness.
