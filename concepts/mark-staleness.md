# Mark Staleness

The risk that portfolio company valuations in BDC books (particularly Level 3 marks) reflect conditions at the last valuation date rather than current market conditions. The gap between mark date and current date creates unreflected gains or losses that may materially misstate NAV.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Staleness is the primary driver of P/NAV discount uncertainty in BDC analysis; a BDC trading at 0.70x P/NAV may be at 0.85x on adjusted marks.
