# Unitranche Subordination

The hidden risk that unitranche loans — reported as "first lien" in BDC filings — contain a "last out" tranche that is structurally subordinated to the "first out" piece held by another lender. The "first lien" classification overstates true recovery seniority.

**Why it matters in this framework:** When 50%+ of a BDC's first lien book is unitranche, the reported seniority profile materially overstates actual recovery in a default scenario.
