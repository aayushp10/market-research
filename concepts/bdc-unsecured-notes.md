# BDC Unsecured Notes

Investment-grade-rated senior unsecured bonds issued by BDCs, structurally subordinated to secured revolvers and CLO facilities. These are the primary tradeable credit expression for BDC risk. Recovery in stress depends on portfolio asset quality, leverage headroom, and the integrity of Level 3 marks above the unsecured tranche.

**Why it matters in this framework:** BDC unsecured notes are the instrument Aayush trades; every issuer-page metric ultimately feeds into spread and recovery assessment for these bonds.
