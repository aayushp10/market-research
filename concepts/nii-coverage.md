# NII Coverage

The ratio of net investment income to the declared dividend, measuring whether a BDC's recurring earnings cover its payout. Coverage below 1.0x means the BDC is paying dividends from spillover income, capital gains, or return of capital — none of which are sustainable indefinitely.

**Why it matters in this framework:** NII coverage trajectory is the single best leading indicator of dividend cuts in BDCs; declining coverage precedes every major BDC dividend reset.
