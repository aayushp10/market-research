# Redemption Gating

The mechanism by which non-traded BDCs and interval funds limit investor redemptions to a fixed percentage of NAV per quarter (typically 5-7%). When requests exceed the cap, investors are denied withdrawal — creating a liquidity mismatch between the vehicle's illiquid assets and investor expectations.

**Why it matters in this framework:** Gating events are the primary stress transmission channel for non-traded vehicles; they signal either real portfolio concerns or sentiment-driven runs that may become self-fulfilling.
